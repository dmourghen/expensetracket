Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the experience.
  • Device information — browser type and operating system, collected automatically for compatibility purposes.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To authenticate your account and keep your data secure.
  • To display your expense records, summaries, and reports within the app.
  • To send transactional emails such as password reset notifications.
  • To identify and fix bugs and improve overall app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating this service. We do not sell your data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take the following measures to protect it:

  • Passwords are hashed using a secure one-way algorithm and are never stored in plain text.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Access to the database is restricted to authorised personnel only.
  • We perform regular backups to prevent data loss.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the product. Each handles data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces. Google may collect limited browser data when fonts are requested.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that collect personally identifiable information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, want to request access to your data, or would like to delete your account, please reach out to us.

You can also review our Terms and Conditions for more information about how the service operates.
